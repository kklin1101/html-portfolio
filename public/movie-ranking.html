The Best Movie According to Katie

My top 3 movies of all-time

About Time

It's a movie with so many layers, not just about cheesy romance but famliy relationships.

Notting Hill

It's a classic romance. Whenever I feel down or there's no hope in finding love, it's my go-to movie to cheer myself up.

I Love You Man

Funny, classic and maybe a bit cheesy, but still a good one to cheer you up when you just need a good laugh.
